Telunjuk.com is a brand under PT. Index Computing Indonesia, a company engaged in the field of technology especially in the field of internet search engines and comparison product (Search Engine and Comparison Portal). At first, telunjuk.com is a product used for personal purposes. Since November 2011, telunjuk.com incubated by the Eden Project, tech-startup accelerator in Indonesia, and began to develop its services in order to be easily used by the people of Indonesia who rely on the Internet for shopping.
