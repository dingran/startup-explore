Digital Workforce is an IT company that provides crowdsourcing solutions for digital media, advertising, database management, and other related fields. It provides multimedia categorization, advertising campaigns on social media, database creation, and builds a consumer base for a startup, project, or product.
It also provides content moderation, product matching and attribute identification, search relevance, data categorization, sentiment analysis, and more.
Digital Workforce is based in Bucharest, Romania.
