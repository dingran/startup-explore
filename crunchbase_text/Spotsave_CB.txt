SpotSave is a hyper-localized mobile promotions app focused on connecting consumers and businesses within their local area. Our mission at Spotsave is to provide local businesses instant access to local potential customers by offering various promotions. Consumers can access local promotions within their local area instantly, share promotions and save for future use. By utilizing the Spotsave app, local businesses will now have an eco-friendly marketing strategy to increase sales and customer retention.
