Situations is the easiest way to let your phone to adapt your life!
Every day, we use our phones to change its behavior. In fact, it should change its behavior on it's own based on what we do and where we are.
With Situations you can 
- Switch to silent when you're in a meeting 
- Open navigation and be descreet when you're driving 
- Extend its battery life when your're not using it 
- Reply your calls with message when you're not able to answer 
- Open Music Player when you connect your headphones 
- Receive SMS when your kids are leaving school and arriving at home. 
- And much more!
Situations is an app that makes your phone do things for you!
