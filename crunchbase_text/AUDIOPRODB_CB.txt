AudioProDB.com is a searchable, online public database, that provides the latest statistics, analytics, and in-depth information on the people and products of the music and audio post- production businesses. Users can create a profile page (“Basic”) free of charge for the central purpose of listing his or her credits and from jobs and projects for which they have worked. For a nominal monthly fee, audio professionals can upgrade their free account to add more detailed and in-depth information (“Pro”), affording them additional site features. It also gives them the ability to network with other professionals on the site.
