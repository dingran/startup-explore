Laboratorio para La Ciudad, or else, The Laboratory for the City, is a project launched to develop the City of Mexico. It is focused on approaching issues of the city through multidisciplinary civic innovation and urban creativity. It is also engaged in creating dialogues and collaborations between the government, civil society, private sector, and NGOs in order to reinvent the city government partially or as a whole.
It strategically promotes creative capital and talent citizens of the City of Mexico through its projects. It develops projects under various categories such as Open City, City Pedestrian, Urban Creativity, and more.
