VAST MEDIA is a media research and consulting company based in Berlin. The company provides international television industry leaders with qualitative competitive market analysis of digital entertainment and content marketing. Tracking and benchmarking broadcasters’ and producers’ digital output worldwide, VAST MEDIA helps their clients discover unique storytelling, TV brand extensions and audience engagement campaigns across all digital platforms.
Sharing the passion of its clients and partners for innovation at the intersection of on air and online, VAST MEDIA is constantly on the lookout to provide its clients with case studies of the most unique and most creative new projects in digital television and storytelling.
VAST MEDIA's team consits of dedicated media aficionados with a background in broadcasting, format development, online media, research and consulting – supported by a growing network of international freelancers. Matthias Puschmann and Henner Herwig Jürgens are heading VAST MEDIA.
