Ethosh Designs is a visual media company. They specialize in simplifying information through visual communication. it offers visual interactive communication solutions using technology that impact vital touch points of an organization, such as sales and marketing, support and service; and learning and development. Their technical communication solutions help companies achieve service effectiveness, accelerated response time, and increased customer satisfaction.
The company offers services and products such as product marketing videos, exhibition interactive display videos, sales specific visualization solutions, assembly and dismantling learning modules, sales toolkits, guided visual troubleshooting communication, interactive service; and maintenance communications. 
Ethosh Designs was founded by Jitendra Dahale and Rahul Deshpande in 2011; and is headquartered in Los Angeles, California.
