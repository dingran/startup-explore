Ikonomo is a free online grocery price comparison and savings service allowing consumers to create a shopping list, comparison shop grocery prices, sales, and coupons to help them save time and money on groceries.
Ikonomo makes it quick and easy for users to create a shopping list and find the store with the lowest total bill for their lists. The users can also browse deals and print online coupons automatically with their lists.
Ikonomo was launched in 2013 by Gregory Fry and Eva Vasilas. It is based in Golden, C.O.
