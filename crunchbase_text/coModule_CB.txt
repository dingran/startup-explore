coModule is a revolutionary platform designed for light electric vehicles. It relieves range anxiety by monitoring battery parameters and displaying the information on the user's smartphone and wearables. The intuitive application allows the user to make routings while sending usage data to the OEM. The OEM can collect data for better development decision and has direct marketing access to all users. coModule strives to become the andoird of electric vehicles by bringing the technology of premium electric cars to light electric vehicles.
