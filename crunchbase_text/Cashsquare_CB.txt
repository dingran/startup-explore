What is Cashsquare?
Cashsquare is an exciting new location-based gaming app that brings the most loved board-game features into the real world on your mobile phone and lets you experience an entirely new way of entertainment.
Cashsquare allows you to buy a tremendous variety of real venues from foursquare and earn rent as people check in at your venues in real-time!
