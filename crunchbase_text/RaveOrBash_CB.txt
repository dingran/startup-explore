RaveOrBash is a social network for consumers to share and discover all consumer experiences with local/non-local businesses, brands and places in a meaningful way that helps without the blemish of fake reviews.
It's a trustworthy platform for connecting consumers around the places, brands and businesses they love through the authentic experiences they share about them.
We recognize the valued-efforts/contributions of our users by rewarding top contributors and users with real rewards they can use.
